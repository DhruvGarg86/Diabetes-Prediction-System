Predict Diabetes

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic medical condition that occurs when the body is unable to properly regulate blood sugar (glucose) levels.

Glucose is a crucial source of energy for cells, and insulin, a hormone produced by the pancreas, helps facilitate the uptake of glucose into cells for energy.

Effect of Diabetes

Diabetes increases the risk of heart disease and stroke due to blood vessel damage and poor circulation.

Diabetes can cause tingling, numbness, or pain in the extremities, affecting nerves and bodily functions.

Diabetes might potentially leading to failure.

What is Type 1 Diabetes?

Type 1 is an autoimmune disorder where the immune system mistakenly attacks and destroys insulin-producing beta cells in the pancreas.

Due to the destruction of insulin-producing cells, individuals with Type 1 diabetes have little to no insulin production.

What is Type 2 Diabetes?

Type 2 is characterized by insulin resistance, where the body's cells do not respond effectively to insulin.

Unlike Type 1 diabetes, there is still some insulin production in Type 2, but it's often insufficient.

Formerly known as adult-onset diabetes
